Pastured Raised Lamb & Beef

Our philosophy is to allow cattle and sheep to do what they were meant to do — graze. We believe this is the best way to provide a quailty product while providing a quality life for our livestock. The animals we raise are rotated on our pastures throughout the year.

We have a herd of beef cattle and a small flock of sheep on our farm in Southern Missouri. If you’re interested in buying our products, we sell the following:

  • Cuts of lamb meat
  • Quarters and halves of beef
  • Live animals, if you’re looking to raise your own

All of our meat is butchered and processed at a local, USDA-inspected facility. For more information, give us a call at 417-259-7992 or email us at info@wm-farms.com.
